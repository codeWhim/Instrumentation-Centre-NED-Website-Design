Electrical Fault Finding & Troubleshooting Training

Course Code: IC-EFF-01
Duration: 2 Days
Date & Timing: On-demand
Venue: NED University of Engineering & Technology
Investment: on-quote
About the Program:

Covers use of schematic diagrams, determining sequence of operation, and use of building diagrams and single-line diagrams.Includes troubleshooting procedures for control circuits and combination starters. Explains troubleshooting practices on DC and AC motors, identifying unmarked leads on three-phase delta and Y-connected motors, and troubleshooting lighting system.

Who should attend:
  • Engineers
  • Technicians
  • Engineering Students
